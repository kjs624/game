TACTICAL DEFENSE PROTOCOL

1. CORE OBJECTIVE

Defend the central Navigation Engine Core at all costs. If its HP drops to 0, the station collapses and the game ends. HP does not regenerate passively; you must perform Emergency Repairs using Gray Vorn Fragments between waves.

2. 360-DEGREE DEFENSIVE POSITIONS

Deploy autonomous drones into concentric ring cells. Drones can only be placed on the **INNER RING** (18 slots) or the **MID RING** (36 slots).

  • Railgun Drone (Sniper): Attacks the highest HP VORN enemy. Pierces linearly. Single-target priority.
  • Plasma Spreader (AoE): Fires explosive orbs towards dense enemy clusters. Slow speed, medium splash range.
  • Shield Node (Barrier): Absorbs Vorn attacks, slows enemies in adjacent areas, and blocks movement towards the Core.

3. CLASS TRINITY LOCKED SYNERGY

Deploying at least one Sniper, one AoE Spreader, and one Barrier Node adjacent to each other in a triangle configuration triggers a **TRINITY LOCK**. Locked units share a mutual HP pool, gain +40% fire rates and stat efficiency, and glow with a connection shield beam.

4. ALIEN TECH REVERSE SYNTHESIS

Slain VORN nanobots drop colored technology shards: Gray (Common), Blue (Uncommon), Purple (Rare), and Gold (Legendary). Click a shard in your synthesis bar, then click any active drone on the grid to fuse modifications onto it! Each unit can hold up to 2 mods. Golden Legendary mods cannot coexist with any other mods.

5. MANUAL SYSTEM ABILITIES

Use accumulated Energy Core Cells to emit manually activated counter-signals: EMP fields to freeze swarms, Overcharges to double attack speed, Recall units for adjustments, or deploy the invincibility Emergency Matrix.
